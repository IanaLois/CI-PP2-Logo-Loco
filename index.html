How to play

Choose the correct answer from 4 options before the timer runs out.

Correct answers will get you 5 points. Incorrect answers will cost you 5 points. If timer expires, you lose 10 points!

There are 20 questions to answer at each difficulty level.

Easy mode has a 10 second timer, Medium has an 8 second timer, and Hard only has 5 seconds.

Good luck and go Logo Loco!
